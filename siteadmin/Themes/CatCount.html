Recount Explaination
Starting a recount of the items on your site will correct any mis-counts on the category list section of the Index, ViewCat and Gallery pages.
These counts should maintain themselves automatically so you won't need to do this very often, but there is always the possibility that something unforeseen could happen and the counts could get out of sync, such as if you added a listing directly to the database or a listing is created in the middle of a database move, etc.
Recounting will count all active items in each category and it's subcategories.
