Promo Code VS. Coupons?  
What's the difference between Promo Codes and Coupons?
Promo Codes are generally used to make larger changes to a users account by doing things adding them to user group or making a deposit in their account.
Coupons are used for basically one-time-shots for a listing. They are entered before a user starts an item to give a discount on that listing alone.
